# Deploy to Netlify - FASTEST Method (2 Minutes)

## Instant Deployment Steps

### Step 1: Get Your Files Ready
Download all files from this package to a folder on your computer

### Step 2: Go to Netlify
1. Open: https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. No account needed for first deploy!

### Step 3: Deploy
1. Drag the ENTIRE FOLDER onto the Netlify Drop zone
2. Wait 30 seconds
3. **DONE! Your site is LIVE!**

### Your Instant URL:
```
https://random-name-12345.netlify.app
```

---

## Make it Permanent (Optional - 2 more minutes)

### Create Account (Free)
1. Click "Sign up" on Netlify
2. Use email or GitHub
3. Claim your deployed site

### Customize URL
1. Site Settings → Change site name
2. Choose: `renukalawassociates`
3. New URL: `https://renukalawassociates.netlify.app`

### Add Custom Domain (If you own one)
1. Domain Settings → Add custom domain
2. Follow DNS instructions
3. Free SSL automatically enabled!

## Enable Contact Form (Netlify Forms)

In `contact.html`, change the `<form>` tag to:

```html
<form name="contact" method="POST" data-netlify="true">
```

Form submissions will appear in your Netlify dashboard!

**FREE**: 100 submissions/month
**Paid**: Unlimited submissions for $19/month
