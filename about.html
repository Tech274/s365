About the Advocate

Advocate Renuka Rao

Professional Enrollment

Advocate Renuka Rao is an enrolled advocate with the Bar Council of Karnataka and is entitled to practice law in accordance with the provisions of the Advocates Act, 1961.

Courts of Practice

Advocate Renuka Rao appears before various courts and tribunals in Karnataka, including subordinate courts, district courts, and other judicial forums as permitted under law.

Areas of Legal Practice

The practice encompasses matters relating to family law, criminal law, property and real estate disputes, protection of women and child rights, civil litigation, and consumer protection matters.

Professional Conduct

The practice is conducted in accordance with the professional standards of conduct, etiquette, and accountability prescribed by the Bar Council of India and applicable legal provisions.
