Legal Practice Areas

The following information describes the areas of law in which legal services are rendered. This information is provided for general awareness and educational purposes only.

Family Law

Family law pertains to legal matters arising from familial relationships, including marriage, divorce, maintenance, child custody, guardianship, adoption, and succession matters. Legal proceedings in family law are governed by various personal laws applicable in India, including the Hindu Marriage Act, Special Marriage Act, and other relevant statutes.

Matters handled include dissolution of marriage, maintenance applications, custody disputes, matrimonial remedies, and matters concerning parental rights.

This information is for general awareness and does not constitute legal advice.

Criminal Law

Criminal law encompasses representation in matters involving offenses punishable under the Indian Penal Code, 1860, and other special criminal statutes. Legal assistance includes representation before magistrate courts, sessions courts, and higher judicial forums in criminal proceedings.

Matters include filing and defending criminal complaints, bail applications, trial proceedings, appeals, and matters involving procedural criminal law.

This information is for general awareness and does not constitute legal advice.

Property & Real Estate Law

Property and real estate law involves legal matters concerning immovable property, including title disputes, ownership claims, partition suits, property transactions, tenancy matters, and enforcement of property rights.

Legal services include examination of title documents, dispute resolution concerning property ownership, matters under the Transfer of Property Act, registration matters, and litigation arising from property disputes.

This information is for general awareness and does not constitute legal advice.

Women & Child Rights

This area of practice focuses on protection and enforcement of legal rights guaranteed to women and children under the Constitution of India and various protective legislations.

Matters include proceedings under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005, matters concerning dowry prohibition, child welfare proceedings, and enforcement of rights under protective statutes enacted for the welfare of women and children.

This information is for general awareness and does not constitute legal advice.

Civil Litigation

Civil litigation encompasses disputes between parties concerning civil rights, obligations, and remedies available under civil law. This includes suits for specific performance, injunctions, declarations, recovery, damages, and other civil remedies.

Representation is provided in civil proceedings before competent courts and tribunals having jurisdiction over such matters.

This information is for general awareness and does not constitute legal advice.

Consumer Law

Consumer law pertains to protection of consumer rights and redressal of consumer grievances under the Consumer Protection Act and related legislation. Legal assistance includes representation before consumer disputes redressal forums and commissions.

Matters include deficiency in service, defective goods, unfair trade practices, and enforcement of consumer rights as provided under applicable consumer protection legislation.

This information is for general awareness and does not constitute legal advice.
